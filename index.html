We can Help.

CogniCor is the provider of automated complaint resolution service. The AI based technology minimizes the need for human intervention in resolving complaints.






Our Products

iResolve

Resolve Complaints Automatically with our State of the Art AI Technology

iComplaint

Single point of Communication between your Organization and its Customers.

iReport

Identify Business patterns and get valuable Information that supports your Business' growth.

‹ ›

iComplaint

If you don’t have an existing front end that interfaces to your client, then iComplaint is the off-the-shelf solution for you. Easy to integrate to your website and resolve complaints from day one.

iResolve

iResolve is the service that resolves simple complaints cost effectively, learning from the past resolutions. You can train iResolve from past complaints data if you have, which means quicker resolution from day one.

iMediate

If you need software assisted negotiation to settle claims from your clients or to retain them with counter offers, then iMediate is the solution for you.

iReport

Get valuable information from your complaints. Learn more about the weaknesses in your system. Stop future complaints from arising by fixing the root cause of complaints from iReport.
